Frequently Asked Questions

Open Entreprenote, dump the mess, and let Charlie help you turn it into something real — one note at a time.

What is Entreprenote?

+

Entreprenote is an AI-powered workspace for the idea in your head. It asks you structured, guided questions that help you clarify, stress-test, and organize a raw idea into an actionable plan — before you spend time or money building.

How do I validate a startup idea with Entreprenote?

+

You start by dumping your raw idea into the app. AI-powered sticky notes help you organize your thoughts, and guided follow-up questions stress-test your problem statement, target audience, and assumptions. You end up with a structured breakdown of what's strong, what needs work, and what to do next.

Is Entreprenote free?

+

Yes. The Free tier includes all core Entreprenote features, For AI you need to BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) or more specifically a Gemini API key and Charlie will start working. Take analogy of a remote control toy car. That's Charlie AI, we have assembled everything, you just put batteries and it'll do it's magic. Batteries is the API key that you put in the idea settings.

Who is Entreprenote for?

+

Entreprenote is built for founders and entrepreneurs at the idea stage — anyone with an idea still in their head who wants clarity on what to do next, before building a product or writing a business plan. Entreprenote helps you in many ways, one of the ways is the help in apply online for accelerators and incubators. It helps you filling out the answers to the questions asked in the application form.
